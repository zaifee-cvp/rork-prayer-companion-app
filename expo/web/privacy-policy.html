Privacy Policy

Prayer Companion: Quran & Qibla  ·  Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Prayer Companion is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. We do not sell, share, or monetise your personal information.

Data We Collect

  • Location data — Your device location is used solely to calculate prayer times and determine Qibla direction. Location is processed on-device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • App settings & preferences — Your chosen city, calculation method, notification preferences, and other settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Dhikr history — Your dhikr counter history is stored locally on your device only.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration or personal identity information
  • No browsing history or usage analytics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data is stored on our servers

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service we use is RevenueCat, which handles in-app subscription management (Premium plan). RevenueCat may collect a device identifier and purchase transaction data to verify and manage your subscription. This is governed by RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Permissions

  • Location — Used only for prayer time calculation and Qibla compass. Never stored remotely.
  • Notifications — Used to deliver prayer time reminders you have opted into. You can disable these at any time in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Prayer Companion does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. The app contains no features that require personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
